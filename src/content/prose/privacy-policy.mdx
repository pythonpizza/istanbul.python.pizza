## <span class="text-primary font-mono">01</span> Who is responsible for your data

The organizers of Python Pizza Istanbul act, jointly and as natural persons ("gerçek kişi"), as the Data Controller ("veri sorumlusu") for personal data processed in connection with the Event, within the meaning of KVKK Article 3. The current organizers are named on our <a href="/#organizers">Organizers</a> page.

If you have any question about your data, or want to exercise any of the rights listed in Section 06, you can reach us at <a href="mailto:organizers@pythonturkiye.org">organizers@pythonturkiye.org</a>. This is also the correct address to use for any KVKK-related request.

</section>

## <span class="text-primary font-mono">02</span> What we process, why, and on what legal basis

<div class="grid gap-6 mt-6">
  <div class="prose-callout">
    <p class="mb-1"><strong>Ticket sales and event admission</strong></p>
    <p class="m-0">Name, email, and payment-related data submitted at checkout through TBA. Processed because it's necessary for the performance of the ticket purchase contract (KVKK Art. 5/2-c).</p>
  </div>
  <div class="prose-callout">
    <p class="mb-1"><strong>Communicating with participants</strong></p>
    <p class="m-0">Email correspondence about the Event (schedule changes, logistics, confirmations), handled through Zoho Mail. Processed on the basis of our legitimate interest in running the Event and keeping participants informed (KVKK Art. 5/2-f).</p>
  </div>
  <div class="prose-callout">
    <p class="mb-1"><strong>Managing proposals (Call for Proposals)</strong></p>
    <p class="m-0">Name, email, and talk/bio details submitted through our CfP form (Google Forms and Google Drive). Processed on the basis of our legitimate interest in reviewing and organizing the Event programme (KVKK Art. 5/2-f).</p>
  </div>
  <div class="prose-callout">
    <p class="mb-1"><strong>Publishing the Event programme</strong></p>
    <p class="m-0">Accepted speakers' names, talk titles, and abstracts, published on our website and its public GitHub repository. We ask for your explicit, separate consent (KVKK Art. 5/1) before publishing this, after you've confirmed your acceptance as a speaker.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="prose-callout">
    <p class="mb-1"><strong>Talk recordings</strong></p>
    <p class="m-0">Video of your talk, if recorded and published on YouTube. Processed only with your separate explicit consent (KVKK Art. 5/1), obtained ahead of the recording.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="prose-callout">
    <p class="mb-1"><strong>Keeping participants safe</strong></p>
    <p class="m-0">Information related to Code of Conduct reports or safety incidents at the Event. Processed on the basis of our legitimate interest in the safety of attendees (KVKK Art. 5/2-f), and, where relevant, our legal obligations as an event organizer (KVKK Art. 5/2-ç).</p>
  </div>
  <div class="prose-callout">
    <p class="mb-1"><strong>Dietary, accessibility, and health-related needs</strong></p>
    <p class="m-0">If you choose to share this information at checkout (for example, for catering or venue accommodations), it's a special category of personal data under KVKK, so we only process it with your explicit consent (KVKK Art. 6). Sharing it is entirely optional and isn't required to complete your ticket purchase. It's kept internal to the organizing team, not shared with caterers or venues, and deleted shortly after the Event once logistics are finished.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="prose-callout">
    <p class="mb-1"><strong>Website analytics</strong></p>
    <p class="m-0">We use GoatCounter to see aggregate visit statistics (like page views and browser type). GoatCounter doesn't use cookies and doesn't store IP addresses or any other identifier, so this doesn't involve processing your personal data.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="prose-callout">
    <p class="mb-1"><strong>Newsletter</strong></p>
    <p class="m-0">If you opt in to hear about future editions of the Event, we use your email address for that purpose alone. This is entirely optional, processed only with your separate, explicit consent (KVKK Art. 5/1), and isn't required to buy a ticket, submit a proposal, or attend.</p>
  </div>

## <span class="text-primary font-mono">05</span> How long we keep your data

Most of the data described above is held on our behalf by the platforms listed in Section 04, and is retained according to their own retention policies. Anything we hold directly (for example, a list of ticket holders for check-in) is kept only for as long as needed for the purpose it was collected for, and deleted once that purpose is fulfilled, unless we're required to keep it longer by law.

Code of Conduct and safety-related records are kept only for as long as needed to address the specific report and any follow-up, and are not retained beyond that without a specific reason.

Dietary, accessibility, and health-related information is deleted shortly after the Event, once catering and venue logistics are finished.

</section>

## <span class="text-primary font-mono">06</span> Your rights

Under KVKK Article 11, you have the right to:

- Learn whether we're processing your personal data, and if so, request information about it
- Learn the purpose of processing, and whether your data is used in line with that purpose
- Know which third parties, in Turkey or abroad, we share your data with
- Request correction of your data if it's incomplete or inaccurate
- Request deletion or destruction of your data where the legal grounds for processing it no longer apply, and ask us to notify any third party we shared it with of that deletion
- Object to a decision made about you based solely on automated processing of your data
- Claim compensation if you're harmed by unlawful processing of your data
- Where a specific processing activity relies on your consent, withdraw that consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at <a href="mailto:organizers@pythonturkiye.org">organizers@pythonturkiye.org</a>. We'll respond within 30 days, free of charge unless the request involves a cost, in which case any fee will follow the tariff set by the Personal Data Protection Board.

If you're not satisfied with our response, you can also file a complaint with Turkey's <a href="https://www.kvkk.gov.tr" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Personal Data Protection Authority (Kişisel Verileri Koruma Kurumu)</a>.
